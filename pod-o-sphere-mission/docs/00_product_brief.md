# 00 Product Brief

## Product name

Pod-o-Sphere

## One-liner

Pod-o-Sphere turns podcast and YouTube archives into branded, searchable topic portals with transcripts, summaries, tags, timelines, and future RAG/chat exploration.

## Target customer

Podcast hosts, YouTube creators, niche experts, churches, educators, interview shows, local media, and organizations with long-form spoken content.

## Initial data source

YouTube channel ingestion.

Future data sources:

- RSS podcast feeds
- Manual audio/video upload
- Vimeo
- Spotify/Apple podcast metadata
- CSV import

## Core user story

As a creator, I want to connect my YouTube channel, let the system process my archive, and publish a branded portal where visitors can search episodes by topic, tag, guest, question, timestamp, and relevance.

## MVP wedge

The first sellable version should focus on:

1. Creator onboarding with YouTube channel URL.
2. Episode/video discovery.
3. Transcript collection through a transcription API.
4. AI summaries, tags, topics, key questions, and timestamped segments.
5. Brandable public portal.
6. Search results that return episode cards, thumbnails, relevant excerpts, timestamps, and tags.

## Differentiators

- Not merely transcription.
- Not merely SEO summaries.
- Branded searchable knowledge base for spoken content.
- Topic timeline across years of episodes.
- Future chatbot grounded in the creator's own archive.

## Product surfaces

### Brochure site

Public marketing site with pricing, examples, creator benefits, demo portal, and onboarding CTA.

### Super Admin backend

Internal Pod-o-Sphere operations console.

### Client Admin backend

Creator/show owner console for brand configuration, source connection, processing status, portal settings, and billing.

### Public portal

The audience-facing searchable archive.
